Bio

I am a first-year PhD student in Financial Economics at ETH Zurich, affiliated with the KOF Swiss Economic Institute. My research interests are money creation in modern financial systems and its implications for central bank policy and financial markets. I hold an M.Sc. in Mathematics from ETH Zurich and a B.Sc. in Engineering Mathematics from KTH Royal Institute of Technology, with a technical background in probability theory and random conformal geometry.
